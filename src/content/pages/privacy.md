## Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

At factualSearch.news, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

### Our Privacy Commitment

**The short version:** We don't track you, we don't profile you, and we don't sell your data. Your searches are your business.

### What We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect or store:

- **Personal identification information** (name, email, phone, etc.)
- **User accounts** (we don't require registration)
- **Search history** tied to your identity
- **Location data** beyond what your browser automatically sends
- **Cookies for tracking purposes**

### What Information We Handle

#### Google Services

We use two Google services that may collect data:

**Google Custom Search Engine**
- Your search queries are processed by Google's search infrastructure
- Google may collect search terms as part of their service
- We do not have access to individual search queries
- See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details

**Google Analytics** (if enabled)
- Anonymous usage statistics (page views, browser type, device type)
- Data is aggregated and not linked to individuals
- Used only to improve site performance and user experience
- You can opt out using browser extensions like [Privacy Badger](https://privacybadger.org/)

#### Local Storage

We use browser local storage for:

- **Caching news feed data** (refreshes weekly)
- **Caching source database** (refreshes weekly)
- This data never leaves your device

#### Server Logs

Our hosting provider may automatically log:

- IP addresses (for security and abuse prevention)
- Browser type and version
- Referring pages
- Timestamps of visits

These logs are used only for:
- Preventing abuse and ensuring site security
- Debugging technical issues
- They are not used for marketing or profiling

### Third-Party Links

Our site links to external content:

- **Search results** link to third-party news websites
- **Source profiles** link to Media Bias/Fact Check
- **News articles** in our feed link to MBFC

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

### Cookies

We use **minimal cookies**:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Session cookies | Site functionality | Browser session |
| Analytics cookies | Anonymous statistics (Google) | Up to 2 years |

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

### Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures:

- HTTPS encryption for all connections
- Regular security updates
- Minimal data collection reduces risk

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Use this service at your own risk.

### Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there's no personal data to:

- Access
- Delete
- Export
- Correct

If you believe we have your personal data and want it removed, contact us via our [About page](/about/).

### Children's Privacy

This service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us immediately.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of changes.

### Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please reach out through our [About page](/about/).
